Homemade Pizza Sauce

Recipe

This is my Go to Sauce for homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • 20 grams Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 8 Grams fine Sea Salt
  • 0.2 gram Dried Oregano
  • 0.4 gram Red Pepper Flakes
  • 28 oz Can Whole Tomatoes

Preparation

  1. Combine Olive Oil, Garlic, Salt,Oregano and Pepper Flakes into blender.
  2. Add one spoonful of Tomatoes
  3. Blend quickly with one ot two pulses to combine
  4. Add the rest of the canned tomatoes and pulse being careful not to over blend over blending with result in watered down sauce
