Recipe

Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • Homemade Dough
  • Homemade Sauce
  • Low Moisture Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Additional toppings

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees and move top rack to the top
  2. Place Dough inside a cast iron oiled cast iron skillet
  3. Add sauce to dough
  4. Sprinkle Mozzarella on top of sauce
  5. Add Parmesan cheese around the edges of the pizza touching the edge
  6. Add any additional toppings
  7. Place Cast Iron on stove top and heat until dough starts to brown
  8. Move cast Iron to oven and bake for 4-8 minutes on top rack
