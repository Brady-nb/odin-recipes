Recipe

Homemade Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 153 grams of 00 flour
  • 153 grams all-purpose flour
  • 8 Grams fine Sea Salt
  • 2 grams active dry yeast
  • 4 grams extra-virgin olive oil
  • 200 grams warm water

Preparation

  1. Combine flour and salt
  2. Stir 200 grams water into 2 grams yeast and 4 grams olive oil
  3. Add flour and salt mixture to water mixture then knead for 3 minutes.
  4. Let rest for 15 minutes
  5. Knead rested dough for 3 minutes
  6. Cut Dough into 2 balls and cover for 3-4 hours.
